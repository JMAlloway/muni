Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate your billing.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Professional and Team plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from this page. Click "Manage / cancel subscription" to access the Stripe portal.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and ACH bank transfers for annual plans.
