CENTRAL OHIO TRANSIT AUTHORITY (COTA)
REQUEST FOR PROPOSALS (RFP) 2024-TSI-08

TRANSIT SUPPORTIVE INFRASTRUCTURE IMPROVEMENTS
CLEVELAND AVENUE BRT CORRIDOR

============================================================
SECTION 1: PROJECT OVERVIEW
============================================================

The Central Ohio Transit Authority (COTA) is seeking qualified contractors to
provide construction services for Transit Supportive Infrastructure (TSI)
improvements along the Cleveland Avenue Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) corridor.

Project Scope:
- Installation of 24 enhanced bus shelters with real-time information displays
- Construction of 12 new concrete bus pads (12' x 60')
- Installation of pedestrian lighting (LED) at all stations
- ADA-compliant sidewalk improvements and curb ramps
- Utility coordination for electrical service to shelters
- Landscaping and site restoration

Project Location: Cleveland Avenue from Downtown Columbus to Westerville Road
Estimated Project Value: $2.8M - $3.2M
Project Duration: 180 calendar days from Notice to Proceed
Substantial Completion Required By: October 15, 2024

============================================================
SECTION 2: MANDATORY REQUIREMENTS
============================================================

2.1 CONTRACTOR QUALIFICATIONS

To be considered responsive, proposers MUST demonstrate:

A. Experience Requirements:
   - Minimum 5 years experience with transit infrastructure projects
   - Completed at least 3 projects involving bus shelter installation in past 5 years
   - At least 1 project with working transit agency within active service areas
   - Experience with FTA-funded projects (Section 5307/5309 grants)
   - ODOT prequalification in: Roadway (R), Concrete (D), Traffic (T)

B. Financial Requirements:
   - Bonding capacity: Performance and Payment bonds at 100% of contract value
   - General Liability Insurance: $2,000,000 aggregate
   - Automobile Liability: $1,000,000 combined single limit
   - Workers Compensation: Statutory limits for Ohio
   - Professional Liability: $1,000,000 (for design-build elements)

C. Certifications:
   - Current Ohio Business Gateway registration
   - OSHA 30-hour certification for superintendent
   - Traffic control certification (ODOT Work Zone certification)
   - Erosion control certification (OEPA)

D. DBE Participation:
   - DBE Goal: 18% of contract value
   - Must demonstrate good faith efforts per 49 CFR Part 26
   - Submit Letter of Intent from DBE subcontractors with proposal

2.2 KEY PERSONNEL REQUIREMENTS

Project Manager: Minimum 7 years managing projects >$2M
Superintendent: Minimum 5 years transit/roadway construction experience
Safety Officer: OSHA 30-hour, minimum 3 years experience
Quality Control Manager: Minimum 5 years, familiar with FTA requirements

Proposers shall submit a comprehensive proposal addressing ALL of the following:

QUESTION 1: Describe your firm's experience with transit infrastructure projects.
Include specific examples of bus shelter installations, pedestrian improvements,

QUESTION 2: How does your firm ensure safety when working in active transit

SECTION 4: EVALUATION CRITERIA
============================================================

Proposals will be evaluated on the following weighted criteria:

Technical Qualifications (30 points)

- Key personnel qualifications
- Safety record

Similar Project Experience (25 points)
- Relevance and complexity
- Client references
- Lessons learned

Project Approach (25 points)
- Technical methodology
- Schedule feasibility
- Risk management

DBE Participation (10 points)
- Commitment to goals
- Specific commitments
- Good faith efforts

Safety & QC Plans (10 points)
- Comprehensiveness
- Transit-specific considerations

TOTAL: 100 points

Shortlisted firms (minimum score: 70) will be invited to interviews.
Final selection will consider technical score (70%) and cost (30%).
